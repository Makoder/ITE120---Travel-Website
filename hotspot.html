10 More Recommended Things to Do

  1. Take a sunset cruise on the Chao Phraya River.
  2. Visit the Erawan Shrine for a spiritual experience.
  3. Explore Chinatown’s vibrant food scene.
  4. Take a cooking class and learn to make authentic Thai dishes.
  5. Enjoy street food at Yaowarat Road.
  6. Climb the Golden Mount for a panoramic view of the city.
  7. Discover contemporary art at the Bangkok Art and Culture Centre.
  8. Shop luxury brands at ICONSIAM or MBK Center for local goods.
  9. Catch a traditional Thai dance performance at the National Theatre.
  10. Relax and rejuvenate with a Thai massage at a local spa.
